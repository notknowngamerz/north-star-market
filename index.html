Before you share

Good to know.

Are all demos free now?

Yes. This testing version has no buying flow. Open any website and use it locally while you decide on your final products.

Are these real websites or preview images?

They are complete multi-page HTML websites with content and simple interactions. Click “Open website” on a card to view the actual site.

Can I use this without a domain?

Yes, on your own machine at localhost. Other people cannot reach your localhost over the internet. Use hosting or a temporary tunnel to show it remotely.

Can I add payments later?

Yes. For real delivery, add a payment gateway and a secure server webhook that verifies payment before it sends downloads or licence access.

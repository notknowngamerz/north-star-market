Six photo series

Every frame is a world.

Six distinct bodies of work spanning portraiture, landscape, still life, and architecture — each series a deliberate exploration of light, texture, and meaning.

A complete local page in the Aperture & Co website collection.

From the intimacy of a face to the grandeur of an empty road — each series asks a different question of the light.

Our portfolio is organised into six self-contained series, each developed over months of patient observation. Some are born from commissions; others from compulsion.

Every series is available as limited-edition prints and can be licensed for editorial or commercial use.

01

The Quiet Hour

Portraits at golden hour — skin, shadow, and the unguarded moment.

02

Distant Meridians

Landscapes from the edge of the map — roads, ridges, and vanishing points.

03

Arranged Silence

Still life compositions — objects in quiet conversation with the light.

Our classes

Six disciplines.
One studio.

From explosive cardio to deep mindful movement, every class at Kinetic Studio is designed to challenge and transform. Find the format that fits your goals.

Browse all six class types below. Each session is led by a certified coach with real-time form coaching and intensity scaling.

Every class is built on science, led by coaches who care about your progress.

We do not do random workouts. Each class follows a structured progression — warm-up, working sets, cool-down — calibrated to the format and your level.

Whether you are burning calories, building strength, or finding balance, there is a class here that fits. All levels are welcome in every session.

01

HIIT

High-Intensity Interval Training alternates between explosive bursts and active recovery. Torch fat, boost endurance, and push your cardiovascular ceiling in 45 minutes.

Duration: 45 min  ·  Difficulty: Advanced

02

Yoga

Dynamic vinyasa flows that build flexibility, core stability, and mental focus. Not just stretching — this is strength through stillness and movement combined.

Duration: 60 min  ·  Difficulty: All levels

03

Strength

Structured resistance training with barbells, dumbbells, and cables. Compound lifts, progressive overload, and accessory work for real, functional strength.

Duration: 50 min  ·  Difficulty: Intermediate

04

Spin

Indoor cycling with intervals, hill climbs, and rhythm-based riding. Low impact on joints, high impact on your aerobic engine. The lights go down, the music goes up.

Duration: 45 min  ·  Difficulty: All levels

05

Pilates

Mat and reformer-inspired work targeting deep core, pelvic floor, and postural muscles. Precision movement that builds a foundation for everything else you do.

Duration: 55 min  ·  Difficulty: All levels

06

Boxing

Technique-driven boxing conditioning. Jabs, crosses, combos, and footwork on the heavy bag and pads. Build power, coordination, and unshakable confidence.

Duration: 50 min  ·  Difficulty: Intermediate

Dr. Elena Vasquez — Full-Stack Development

Elena spent nine years at two major cloud platforms before joining Lumen. She has shipped backend services handling billions of requests and led the migration of monolithic systems to microservices. She teaches because she believes the gap between academic computer science and production engineering is unnecessary — and that good teaching can close it.

  • ExpertiseBackend architecture, distributed systems, API design
  • Courses taughtModule 01: Computational Thinking, Module 04: Backend & APIs
  • PreviouslySenior engineer, cloud infrastructure (9 years)
  • Based inBarcelona, Spain

Marcus Chen — Frontend Craft

Marcus is a frontend architect who has built design systems used across three continents. He is the kind of developer who can explain why a CSS grid breaks — and make you laugh while doing it. His teaching style is methodical: he demonstrates, then he makes you do it, then he shows you what you missed.

  • ExpertiseCSS architecture, React, accessibility, performance
  • Courses taughtModule 02: Web Fundamentals, Module 03: Frontend Craft
  • PreviouslyFrontend lead, SaaS platform (7 years)
  • Based inVancouver, Canada

Aisha Okafor — Design Systems

Aisha runs the design systems practice at a fintech company and has consulted for teams transitioning from ad-hoc design to systematic thinking. She teaches design systems not as a set of tools but as a discipline — one that requires taste, consistency, and the ability to say no. Her students leave with a system they can actually use.

  • ExpertiseDesign tokens, component APIs, documentation, Figma
  • Courses taughtModule 05: Design Systems
  • PreviouslyDesign systems lead, fintech (6 years)
  • Based inLagos, Nigeria
